Swim School

Runnymede Swim School is one of best in the area with a waiting list of applicants.  It is an important and valued part of the club,
helping people of all ages and abilities learn to swim and providing the club with its next generation of competitors.

We take pupils from the age of 3-years upwards from complete beginners to pupils who wish to improve their strokes.  The swim school runs its lessons at many pools and times in the local area, so there are sessions to suit most needs.

Runnymede Swim School uses the National Plan for Teaching Swimming to encourage a pupil to achieve specific target and to provide rewards for doing so.  All Teachers and Assistant Teachers within the swim school are qualified to the Amateur Swimming Association standards at the required Level 1 and 2.

Pupils naturally progress from the swim school to the club, where the coaching builds on the firm foundations that the school gives them. The transition from school to club is seamless for the pupil and provides a goal to aim for over time.
