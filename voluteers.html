Volunteers Needed

“Coaching Life’s Winners”

Runnymede Swimming Club relies heavily on a small army of volunteers to operate effectively. We are always looking for volunteers to help us out on the committee, as well as helping out as and when needed to run galas, etc.

If you are interested in volunteering to ensure that the club can continue to offer your child/children the best experience during their time at Runnymede, please get in touch with any existing member of the committee.

Current vacancies include …

Vice Chair

Volunteers Coordinator

SwimMark Coordinator

Website and eNewsletter Coordinator

Please contact an existing member of the Committee if you would like to find out more about volunteering for the above vacancy, or if you can help the club out in other ways.
