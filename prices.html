Prices

Keeping the costs down for swimmers and/or their parents is of paramount importance to the Runnymede committee. The expenses associated with running a swimming club are not slight, by any means, and we do our best to raise much of those funds through other avenues, such as fund raising events, grants and scholarships, and sometimes generous donations. However, the bulk of this cost still has to be met by our members.

We recognise that our squads spend different amounts of time in the pool, and as such we have spread the cost more fairly amongst our members based on which squad they are in. Below is a summary of our membership fees…

 

Club Membership Fees

Club Membership – Full Year 1st September to 31st August (Renewal) £65.00
Concessions (Students 16 years + & OAP’s) £23.00
New Club Membership after 1 September reduces by £2 per month, however this is not available for renewals of existing membership
